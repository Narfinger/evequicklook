evequicklook
============
Gives you some information about your eve character:
- ServerTime
- Paid Until Time
- Character Balance
- Transactions from last day
- Market Orders
- Skill Queue


Howto
============
put in your KEY_ID and API_KEY and download sqlite db from
https://www.fuzzwork.co.uk/dump/sqlite-latest.sqlite.bz2
